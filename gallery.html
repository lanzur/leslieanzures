Don't Smile at Me is the debut extended play by American singer-songwriter Billie Eilish. It was released on August 11, 2017 through Interscope Records, and contains several of her previously released singles, including "Ocean Eyes", "Bellyache", "Watch", "Copycat" and "Idontwannabeyouanymore"
